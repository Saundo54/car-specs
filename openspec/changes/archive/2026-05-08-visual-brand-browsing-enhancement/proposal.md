## Why

Currently, the brand-specific model/variant list only shows text-based information. Adding images to this list will help users visually identify vehicles before diving into detailed specifications, improving the discoverability and aesthetic appeal of the prototype.

## What Changes

- Update the vehicle card in the `selectedMake` view of `SearchScreen.tsx` to include the vehicle's hero image.
- Add styling to ensure images are displayed as consistent thumbnails or top-aligned hero banners within the card.
- Maintain the reachability-first layout and clean Material You aesthetic.

## Capabilities

### Modified Capabilities
- `grouped-browsing-ui`: Enhanced with visual elements for vehicle selection.

## Impact

- **Frontend**: UI updates to `SearchScreen.tsx` and `SearchScreen.module.css`.
