## Context

User wants visual confirmation of models in the brand subpage.

## Goals / Non-Goals

**Goals:**
- Add images to vehicle cards in the brand view.
- Ensure cards remain compact but visual.

## Decisions

### 1. Card Layout: Image Top
We will place the hero image at the top of the card, spanning the full width, with text content below.
- **Rationale**: This is a standard Material Design card pattern that prioritizes visual recognition.
